## About Me

I am a third-year PhD candidate at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://en.sjtu.edu.cn/), advised by [Prof. Weidi Xie](https://weidixie.github.io/). I received my Bachelor degree in Information Engineering, also from Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research focuses on **long video understanding** and **multi-modal representation learning**.
